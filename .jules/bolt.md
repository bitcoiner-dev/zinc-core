## 2025-03-30 - O(1) Fast-Path Checks with HashSet Before Linear Searches
**Learning:** In contexts where identifying objects that meet a certain criteria is needed, and those objects are stored in a `Vec` leading to an O(N) linear search, utilizing a pre-populated `HashSet` for an O(1) membership check before performing the linear search acts as a highly effective fast-path. This codebase uses a `HashSet` (`inscribed_utxos`) to check for the presence of an inscription in an output, avoiding the expensive linear iteration over the full vector of `inscriptions`.
**Action:** When working with collections, look for opportunities to pair a slow, detailed search (like filtering a `Vec`) with a fast, broad check (like `HashSet::contains`) to exit early on negatives and significantly improve performance.
